Tips to Identify Fake News

  • Check the source credibility and domain reputation
  • Look for spelling and grammatical errors
  • Verify the publication date
  • Cross-check with other reliable sources
  • Examine images for signs of manipulation
  • Be skeptical of sensational headlines
  • Check the author's credentials
