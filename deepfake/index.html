Common Deepfake Types

Face Swapping

Replacing one person's face with another in images or videos. Commonly used in celebrity deepfakes.

Lip Syncing

Altering video to make it appear someone is saying words they never actually spoke.

Puppet Mastery

Transferring facial expressions from one person to another, creating realistic animated faces.

Voice Cloning

Generating synthetic speech that mimics a specific person's voice with high accuracy.

Current Detection Approaches

  • Visual Artifact Analysis

    Identifying inconsistencies in lighting, shadows, and reflections that deepfake algorithms often miss.

  • Biological Signal Detection

    Analyzing subtle physiological signals like heartbeat and breathing patterns that are difficult to fake.

  • Digital Forensic Analysis

    Examining metadata and compression artifacts to identify manipulation in media files.

  • Behavioral Consistency Checks

    Verifying that facial expressions, gestures, and speech patterns match the person's known behavior.
